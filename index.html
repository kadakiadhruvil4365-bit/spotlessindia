Join This Sunday How It Works

Spotless India Foundation is a grassroots civic movement started in Bharuch, Gujarat. Every Sunday, volunteers gather at one public location, clean it, segregate the waste into five categories, and dispatch it to certified handlers.

We are a Section 8 non-profit. Every rupee is tracked publicly. Every invoice is filed openly. We are building the civic habit India deserves — one Sunday at a time.

What we do

Clean one spot.
Every Sunday.

A simple, repeatable model. Show up, clean up, segregate, dispatch, repeat. Every week. Every city.

01
Show up on Sunday

Every Sunday morning, volunteers gather at one announced public location — a road, market, park, or monument — and get to work.

02
Segregate & dispatch

Waste is sorted into 5 colour-coded categories and dispatched to the right recyclers, e-waste handlers, and the municipal dump yard.

03
Expand city by city

Starting in Bharuch. Every chapter runs under the same Section 8 company. No separate registration. Just a coordinator and a Sunday.

The kit system

5-colour waste segregation

Every volunteer carries a kit. Every piece of waste goes into the right bag. Every bag goes to the right place.

Yellow

Dry recyclables — paper, cardboard, plastics. To GIDC recyclers, Ankleshwar.

Green

Organic / wet waste. To Bharuch municipal dump yard composting.

Red

Hazardous waste — chemicals, broken glass. To hazardous section.

Blue

E-waste — batteries, wires, electronics. To CPCB-authorised handler, Gujarat.

Black

Inert / residual waste. Mixed unusable waste to municipal dump yard.
